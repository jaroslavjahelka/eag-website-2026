Their role is to help the model choose the correct path, stay within scope, and avoid skipping necessary design, planning, validation, or review steps. They turn authority, constraints, and quality standards into repeatable execution paths, so similar tasks are handled consistently and with less ambiguity.

## Definition of strong workflow

A strong workflow is explicit enough to guide execution. It should make clear what happens first, what must be validated before proceeding, when work should be decomposed, when uncertainty must be escalated, and when a handoff to another procedure is required.
