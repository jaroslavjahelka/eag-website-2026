# Design System Alignment Rubric

## Score 1 — Misaligned

Arbitrary values, duplicate primitives, or unclear variant choices bypass the system.

## Score 3 — Partially aligned

The system is mostly respected, but there are avoidable exceptions or weak semantic naming.

## Score 5 — Strongly aligned

Tokens, primitives, variants, and semantics are used coherently with no avoidable drift.
